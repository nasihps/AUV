About our project

Welcome to our project on real-time object detection using TensorFlow.js and MobileNet! Our project aims to demonstrate the capabilities of machine learning in identifying objects in live video streams directly from the user's webcam.

how to use ?

pre-requisites

  • TensorFlow.js
    TensorFlow.js is a library for training and deploying machine learning models on Node.js.

  • MobileNet
    Optimized for speed and efficiency while still maintaining high accuracy in image classification

  • Object detection
    Identify objects in live video streams directly from the user's webcam and prerecorded videos.
  • step 1
    connect your camera output from your AUV to pc via USB cable
  • step 2
    click on live detection and see our model working.
  • step 3
    if you have prerecorded video,click on the second option below
  • 1
    An active internet connection to your pc

  • 2
    live video out from an AUV or prerecorded video

  • 3
    A compatible browser and permissions to use your video for detection
